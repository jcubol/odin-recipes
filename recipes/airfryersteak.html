Air Fryer Steak

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 sirloin steaks, 1 inch thick (1 1/2 lbs total)
  • Olive oil spray

DIRECTIONS

  1. Combine the spices in a small bowl.
  2. Spray the steak with olive oil and coat both sides with the spices.
  3. Preheat the air fryer so the basket gets hot. For a 1-inch steak, air fry 400F 10 minutes turning halfway, for medium rare.
    Time may vary slightly with different air fryer models, and the thickness of the steaks.
  4. Finish with a pinch of more salt and black pepper
  5. Let it rest, tented with foil 5 minutes before slicing.
